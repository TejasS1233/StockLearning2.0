Mutual Funds & ETFs

Learn about diversified funds for easy, long-term portfolio building without the need to pick individual stocks.

What Are They?

Both Mutual Funds and Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs) are professionally managed collections of individual stocks, bonds, and other assets. They offer an excellent way for investors to achieve instant diversification. Instead of buying one or two stocks, you're buying a small piece of many different companies.

Key Differences

Feature Mutual Funds ETFs
TradingOnce a day, after market closeThroughout the day, like a stock
ManagementOften actively managedUsually passively managed (index tracking)
Expense RatioGenerally higherGenerally lower
