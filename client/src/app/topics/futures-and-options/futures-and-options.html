Futures & Options

Understand derivatives and advanced strategies for experienced investors to hedge risks or speculate on market movements.

High Risk Warning

Futures and Options (F&O) are complex financial instruments and involve a high degree of risk. They are not suitable for all investors. A substantial portion of traders lose money in F&O. Proceed with caution and ensure you are fully educated before trading.

Understanding Futures

A futures contract is a legal agreement to buy or sell a particular commodity or financial instrument at a predetermined price at a specified time in the future.

Understanding Options

Options are contracts that give the owner the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an underlying asset at a set price on or before a certain date. A "Call" option is a bet that the price will rise, while a "Put" option is a bet that the price will fall.
